Our Services

Comprehensive Building Support

We provide end-to-end advocacy services designed to protect your interests, save you money, and ensure your home is built to the highest standards.

Design Consultation

Work with experienced architects to create cost-effective custom designs that match your vision, lifestyle, and budget constraints.

Builder Selection

Access our vetted network of Melbourne builders. We compare quotes, capabilities, and reputations to find your perfect match.

Contract Review

Expert analysis of HIA and MBA contracts. We identify risks, unfair clauses, and negotiate better terms on your behalf.

Cost Estimation

Professional quantity surveying and tender preparation. Ensure accurate pricing and avoid budget blowouts before construction begins.

Build Advocacy

Ongoing support during construction. We attend site meetings, review progress claims, and ensure quality and compliance standards.

Practical Completion

Detailed defect inspections and handover advocacy. We ensure your home is finished to specification before final payment.
